HISTORY OF HOLY ANGELS

Introduction : We humbly exhibit ourselves as a school which has been stepping into 35th year of service. We hereby recall our infant growth and our future goal. The Holy Angels Nursing School was started in 1972 by Late Miss. Grace Jacob. This is the first English Medium School in Pallikonda. After the death of Miss. Grace Jacob in 1994 this school was managed by Late Mr: John William till Mr. Benjamin Prabakaran took over the school from 23rd March 1995.

Up gradation : Having the high goal of character building, harmonious development of the physical, mental social and spiritual powers among the children. Our school was upgraded as Matriculation School on 2002 and Higher Secondary School on 2007. To provide service oriented education in and around Pallikonda, we have established Nursery & Primary Schools at Madhanur.

Achievements : Due to the valuable support of the parents, tireless efforts of the teachers, we had produced 100% result in X Matric Public Examinations.
